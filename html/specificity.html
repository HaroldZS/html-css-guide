Importance, specificity and Order of sources

Importance:

  • 1. User agent style sheet (Browser styles)
  • 2. Author style sheet (Css style sheet)
  • 3. Important statements of author style sheet (!important)

Specificity:

Selectors Specificity
!important (1,0,0,0,0)
Inline style (0,1,0,0,0)
#id (0,0,1,0,0)
.class (0,0,0,1,0)
tag (0,0,0,0,1)

Order of sources:

Declarations at the end of the document will override those that come before in case of conflict
